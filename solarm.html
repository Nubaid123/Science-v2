How we Made it: Solar Energy Generator

                                                                              

This exquisite model mainly consists of a solar panel, which will provide the main base and functionality of this model.

A flashlight will be used to simulate sunlight in this model. As the torchlight hits the solar panel. The solar panel is exposed to the photons present in the light and releases electrons and produces an electric charge.

This will be connected via electric wires and the charge will be transmitted to the LED present inside the house attributed to solar energy.

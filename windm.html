How we Made it: Wind Energy Generator

                                                                              

This distinctive model, is prominently composed of various wood products. At the bottom is a tall base. On the sides of the base are wooden stand, upon which a model wind turbine is attached.

Behind the wind turbine is a dynamic hobby motor. As wind blows and the turbine is turned, the motor (which now acts as a generator) converts the mechanical energy, produced by the spinning turbine, into electrical energy.
